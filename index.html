B.T.E. BOTLabs Trusted Entity GmbH (B.T.E.) creates services that enhance and protect digital identity across gaming, health care, IoT, academic, energy and more.

We use decentralised Identifiers (DIDs) and Verifiable Credentials (VCs) anchored on the KILT blockchain.

Your Identity in Your Hands

Credentials to users and then forgets about the transaction. [cut this line, if too long: Users store

KILT & credentials wallet

Sporran is your gateway to the KILT blockchain. It’s used for storing KILT Coins, creating and
holding decentralized digital identities, and signing any transaction on the KILT blockchain.
You can also use Sporran for obtaining and managing off-chain and on-chain DIDs, and
storing and sharing Verifiable Credentials that remain fully under your control.

Born to stake

A staking platform for KILT Coins that integrates with the Sporran wallet. Interact directly with the KILT blockchain to stake your KILT, earn rewards and help secure the network.

About us

B.T.E. BOTLabs Trusted Entity GmbH is a subsidiary of BOTLabs GmbH, a blockchain R&D company founded in 2018 and based in Berlin. BOTLabs GmbH is the initial developer of KILT Protocol, now a fully decentralized blockchain identity network for issuing self-sovereign, anonymous and verifiable credentials.

B.T.E. collaborates with developers, enterprise and government entities to build applications and services that restore user control and protect digital identity. Use cases include gaming, health care, IoT, academia, sustainability and energy.
